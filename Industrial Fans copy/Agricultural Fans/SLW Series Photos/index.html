SLW Series Agricultural Fans

Product Description

The SLW Series agricultural fans are designed to move and replace stagnant air in your building or housing while providing efficient air circulation for a variety of applications, including greenhouses and livestock operations.

Features

  • Rugged heavy gauge galvanized steel frame and housing
  • Single-phase, 230 volt motors with a molded power cord and plug
  • Three-phase motors available
  • Direct drive or belt drive models
  • Optional temperature control (0°F to 100°F)

Discharge Cones

Optional discharge cones are available to direct airflow as needed.
