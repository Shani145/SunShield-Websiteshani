Ceiling Mounted Fans

Moving Air Conveniently

The Ceiling Mounted fans are rugged, low-cost air movers and are available in direct drive models up to 48" and belt drive models up to 84". Yoke mounted models allow for precise, directional cooling of people, products or equipment. They are used worldwide to cool assembly lines, machine shops, loading docks, gymnasiums, warehouses, or anywhere a high volume of air movement is required.

Features

  • Heavy gauge steel barrels
  • Deep pitched blades for maximum airflow
  • OSHA approved spiral wound guard
  • Single and three-phase models available
  • Suspension or yoke mounted models

Product Types

  • CMB - Yoke Mounted Belt Drive Fans
  • CMPC - Yoke Mounted Direct Drive Fans
  • Suspended Gentle Breeze
