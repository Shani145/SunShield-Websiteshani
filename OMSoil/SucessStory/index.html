OM Ag Success Story

Transforming soil health and water efficiency at Costa Mesa Sports Complex

Project Overview

This success story highlights the transformation at a large sports complex in Costa Mesa, California. The soil was treated with OM Ag’s Soil Amendment Solution, resulting in improved soil health, reduced salinity, increased soil moisture, and nutrient boosts, even under challenging conditions with high-pH recycled water.

Key Results

Description 12/8/11 Average 2/28/12 Average Percentage Change
pH 7.68 7.59 -1.17%
Salinity 1.52 1.12 -26.32%
Chloride 210 137 -34.76%
Soil Moisture 63% 69% +9.52%
SAR (Sodium Adsorption Ratio) 7.0 5.8 -17.14%

In under three months, salinity decreased by 26%, chloride by 35%, and soil moisture capacity improved by 10%.

Conclusion

The results from the Costa Mesa project demonstrate that OM Ag’s Soil Amendment Solution not only improves soil quality but also enhances water retention and nutrient availability. These sustainable results were achieved with minimal rainfall and without sacrificing plant health.
